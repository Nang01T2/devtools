About Image Optimizer, Converter and PDF Maker

Convert images between modern and classic formats including AVIF, WebP, JPEG XL (JXL), MozJPEG, PNG, and animated GIF, with full control over quality, resizing, and optimization. It can batch-process many files at once and combine images into a single PDF. Conversion uses WASM codecs that run in your browser, so your files never leave your device.

How to use

  1. Add one or more images, or a batch of files, to convert.
  2. Pick the target format (AVIF, WebP, JXL, MozJPEG, PNG, or animated GIF).
  3. Adjust quality, resizing, and optimization settings to balance size and fidelity.
  4. Optionally combine supported images (PNG, JPG/JPEG, GIF, WebP) into a single PDF.
  5. Process the batch and download your converted files.

Frequently asked questions

Are my images uploaded anywhere during conversion?

No. Conversion runs locally using WASM codecs in your browser, so your files never leave your device.

Which formats can I convert between?

You can convert across AVIF, WebP, JPEG XL (JXL), MozJPEG, PNG, and animated GIF, in either direction.

Can I combine several images into one PDF?

Yes. You can merge supported images into a single PDF; only PNG, JPG/JPEG, GIF, and WebP inputs are eligible for the PDF output.

Can I process multiple images at once?

Yes. Batch processing lets you convert, resize, and optimize many files in one operation.
